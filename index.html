Welcome!

I am currently an Assistant Professor at Coastal Carolina University. My research focuses on sustainable and durable concrete through nanoscience, supplementary cementitious materials, and coastal infrastructure resilience.

Our research uncovers knowledge about sustainable construction materials and develops innovative solutions for coastal infrastructure challenges. We create new materials and systems by developing better simulation, fabrication, and design methods for concrete structures.

About My Work

I specialize in sustainable and durable concrete through nanoscience, supplementary cementitious materials, and coastal infrastructure resilience. My research aims to develop innovative solutions for the construction industry while addressing environmental challenges.
